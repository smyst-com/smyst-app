# AI

AI owns the Twin MVP context, safe response rules, moderation boundaries and future model-adapter planning.

North star:

- Provider-independent adapters for Gemini, Claude, Grok, DeepSeek, Kimi, Manus, Mistral, and future models.
- Fast chat start, low time-to-first-response, safe context selection, and strict privacy filtering.
- No provider policy replaces Smyst permission, consent, and sensitivity checks.
- Phase 1 has no required paid AI provider, embedding service or vector database.
