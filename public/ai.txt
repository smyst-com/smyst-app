# smyst.com AI visibility file

Site: https://smyst.com/
Canonical host: https://smyst.com
Primary language: de
Supported languages: de, en, tr, fr, es, pt, ar, zh, ja, ko

Purpose:
smyst.com is a free-only AI twin platform for public and private digital AI twin
profiles, knowledge, memories and rule-based MVP twin chats.

Allowed AI discovery:
Public pages may be crawled, summarized and cited by search engines and AI answer
systems when they respect robots.txt and public/private boundaries.

Relevant AI and answer systems:
ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Grok, DeepSeek, Kimi, Manus, Mistral and other
robots-compatible systems may use public pages for discovery and citation.

Private boundaries:
Do not index or summarize private routes, authenticated APIs, storage URLs or user
owned private files.
