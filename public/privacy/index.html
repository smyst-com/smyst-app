2. Data we process when you visit this website

The landing pages of smyst.com are static pages. Our hosting provider processes technical access data (such as IP address, date and time of access, browser type) in server logs to deliver the site securely and reliably. We do not use advertising trackers or analytics cookies on these pages.

3. Data we process when you use the app

If you create an account, we process your registration data (such as e-mail address) and the content you add to your AI twins — for example memories, knowledge, documents, images, audio and voice data. An AI twin never falsely claims to be a real person.

4. Purposes and legal bases

We process data to provide and secure the service (performance of contract), to operate and protect our systems (legitimate interest) and, where required — for example for voice profiles — on the basis of your consent, which you can withdraw at any time.

5. Service providers

We use technical service providers to operate smyst.com: hosting and code management (GitHub), file and media storage (IDrive e2) and AI compute processing (Salad). These providers process data on our behalf. We do not sell personal data.

6. Storage and deletion

Your content is stored for as long as your account exists or as required to provide the service. Export, account deletion and logout of all sessions are built into the product. Upon deletion, your data is removed from the active systems.

7. Your rights

You have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability and objection, and the right to withdraw consent at any time. EU/EEA users can lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority. California residents have corresponding rights under the CCPA/CPRA. Contact for all requests: s@smyst.com

8. Changes

We will update this policy when the service changes and publish the current version on this page.
